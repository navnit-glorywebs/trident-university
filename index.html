For Military and Their Families

Trident is a military-centric college that recognizes and, most importantly, respects your service and the sacrifices made daily by your family. We serve all branches of the military, offering scholarships and grants* and a variety of ways to earn the quality education you deserve.

Trident is a Military-Friendly School*

We offer high-quality degrees in a manner that is convenient for military students. Trident stands out among military-centric colleges because of our unwavering commitment to supporting service members and veterans as they pursue higher education.
